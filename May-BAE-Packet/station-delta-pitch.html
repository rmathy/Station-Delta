// The Problem

You know who's brilliant.
You don't always know who's ready.

01

The Readiness Gap

Junior engineers look strong on paper but freeze when something breaks in production. Traditional certifications don't test under real pressure.

02

No Objective Data

Managers make on-call and deployment decisions based on gut feel. In defense environments, that's not just inefficient — it's a liability.

03

The AI Skills Cliff

AI-augmented systems are now standard. Most training programs haven't caught up. Engineers are being handed tools they weren't trained to operate.

Simulation-based training with
