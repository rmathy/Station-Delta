# BAE Slide — Pilot Ask and Success Metrics (Single Slide Copy)

## Title
**Pilot Ask: Validate Readiness Impact in 4–6 Weeks**

## The Ask
We are requesting a scoped pilot collaboration to validate Station Delta in a defense-relevant training lane.

## Proposed Pilot Shape
- Cohort: 10–30 participants
- Duration: 4–6 weeks
- Focus: one track (example: command diagnostics, incident-response workflow, or secure ops fundamentals)
- Delivery: web-based simulation with structured checkpoints

## What We Provide
- Configured scenario path
- Weekly insight summary (fix/clarify/add recommendations)
- Readiness analytics snapshot (baseline, midpoint, final)
- Final pilot report + recommended expansion roadmap

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in time-to-resolution (TTR) on core objectives
- Reduction in hint dependency over pilot duration
- Increased first-attempt correctness on assessed steps
- Identified high-friction objectives with remediation plan

## Close / CTA
**Decision requested:** proceed to technical discovery and pilot scoping workshop.  
**Target timeline:** kickoff within 2 weeks of approval.

## Speaker Notes (20–30 seconds)
"We’re not asking for a large rollout on day one. We’re proposing a measurable pilot with clear success criteria and a short feedback loop. If outcomes are strong, we scale with confidence."
